Our Story

Crafting excellence since the beginning

The Vision Behind ARCOVA

In 2018, two childhood friends reconnected over a shared frustration: finding quality furniture that balanced timeless design with modern functionality seemed impossible. What started as late-night sketches and weekend woodworking sessions transformed into ARCOVA.

Our founders, Arjun Mehta and Kavya Sharma, believed that furniture should tell a storyâ€”of craftsmanship, sustainability, and the people who use it. They spent years traveling across India, learning from master craftsmen, understanding traditional joinery techniques, and exploring innovative metalworking processes.

Today, ARCOVA stands as a testament to their vision: where industrial precision meets artisanal warmth, creating pieces that don't just furnish spaces but transform them into homes.

The Spark of Innovation

How ARCOVA came to life

The idea crystallized during a renovation project when Arjun and Kavya couldn't find furniture that matched their aesthetic vision and quality standards. They realized the market gap: premium furniture was either prohibitively expensive or lacked character. Armed with design expertise and a passion for craftsmanship, they decided to create the brand they wished existed. ARCOVA was born from the simple belief that everyone deserves beautifully made furniture that lasts generations, not just seasons.
